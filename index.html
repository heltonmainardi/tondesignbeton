IARA

CONCRETE WASHBASIN

Wall-mounted Washbasin

Iara, also called lady of the waters, is a Tupi-Guarani goddess (indigenous ethnicity from northern Brazil) who lives in the depths of the Amazon River and attracts men through her beauty and riches. She will be the main feature in any space.

customizable length

Palatial, measuring 22” wide, 4” profile and customizable length between 67” and 100”, Iara surprises for being a rough piece of solid concrete floating on the wall.
This is possible thanks to the Econcrete technology.

67" ~ 100"( 170cm ~ 254cm )
COLOR OPTIONS

Econcrete

Multi-layer concrete. The first layer made of high-strength concrete provides strength and impermeability to the surface. Followed by a layer of glass-fiber-concrete that offers significant flexural strength. The inner part is filled with a special mixture of concrete and polystyrene grains, which allows it to remove a large part of the weight while maintaining maximum structural strength. All polystyrene used in the production of IARA is recycled from the by-product of constructions and packaging.
